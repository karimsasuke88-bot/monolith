Why Your Eyes Hurt After Gaming

Reading Time: 2 Minutes • Health

If you game for hours and end up with a throbbing headache, the real culprit is Contrast Stress. This happens when your eyes struggle to adjust between a bright screen and a dark room.

The Solution: Bias Lighting

You need to raise the ambient light level *behind* your monitor. This reduces the contrast. The easiest way is a Monitor Light Bar.

The Focus Bar

A light bar that shoots light backward, creating a soft halo. It saves your eyes instantly.
