The Curator's Guide

The Misdiagnosis of Poor Stream Quality.

Lighting is often blamed for a grainy camera feed. The actual issue is usually the hardware capturing the light.

The False Security of the Power Strip.

High-end components are protected by software and casings, but remain entirely vulnerable to the electrical current that powers them.

The Negligence of Auditory Hardware.

Visual upgrades reach a point of diminishing returns if spatial awareness is limited by outdated audio.

Why Your Eyes Hurt After Gaming.

It's not just the screen. It's the contrast. Learn the science behind monitor lighting and how to fix headaches instantly.

The Psychology of a Clean Desk.

Visual clutter competes for your brain's attention. Moving cables out of sight frees up mental processing power for your game.

The Foundation of Speed.

A mouse falling off the edge of a small pad ruins a round. Oversized mats provide consistent friction for low-sensitivity gaming.
