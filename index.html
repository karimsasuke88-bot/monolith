Curator's Notes

The myth of the "minimalist" wallet.

We carry too much. Receipts from three years ago, library cards we never use. I switched to a metal card holder two years ago. It forced me to declutter. Now, I only carry what I need.

Why your desk mat matters more than your desk.

Most desks are ugly. They are particle board or scratched wood. A good desk mat covers the sins. It provides the "foundation." It’s the first thing your wrist touches.

The invisible upgrade: Monitor Lighting.

Working in a dark room with a bright screen is a guaranteed way to burn out your eyes. Adding a bias light reduces the contrast strain instantly.
